Dining

Seasonal menus crafted to celebrate the mountains and forests around us.

View Menus Our Philosophy

Fresh, Seasonal, Local

At Moffat Bay, dining blends nature with craftsmanship. Our chef crafts each dish with locally sourced ingredients — from freshly caught fish and farm-raised meats to hand-picked produce — making every plate a celebration of the region’s flavors. With menus that change seasonally, every meal is fresh, authentic, and deeply connected to the land.

Greenhouse to Table

Our culinary team enhances this connection with herbs and greens grown in Moffat Bay’s greenhouse. This dedication to freshness ensures dishes that are vibrant, nourishing, and sustainable. Each meal balances rustic lodge warmth with refined technique, creating dining experiences that are both comforting and unforgettable.

Dining Throughout the Day

From sunrise to evening, Moffat Bay offers dining for every moment of your stay. Begin the day with a hearty breakfast buffet of pastries, fruit, and hot dishes. At midday, our casual café provides light plates and lodge favorites. In the evening, fine dining transforms local ingredients into elegant multi-course dinners, paired with curated wines and stunning views.
