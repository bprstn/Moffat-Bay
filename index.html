Welcome to Moffat Bay

Experience the warmth of a modern forest lodge paired with refined comforts. Our world-renowned chef crafts seasonal menus from locally sourced ingredients and herbs grown in our private greenhouse, while our rooms and suites offer serene views of the lake and mountains.

Easily plan adventures on nearby paths, relax by the fire, and return to cuisine that celebrates the region. Moffat Bay is your quiet base for unforgettable days outdoors and cozy evenings in.
