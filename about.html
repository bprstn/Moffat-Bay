About Moffat Bay Lodge

A retreat where nature, comfort, and hospitality meet.

Our Story

Moffat Bay Lodge began as a vision to create a peaceful haven along the lakeshore. With its roots in the surrounding forest and community, the lodge was built to offer guests a balance of natural beauty and refined comfort. Every stay is meant to feel like a return to simplicity, with the warmth of a modern lodge and the serenity of the outdoors.

Our Mission

To provide every guest with a sanctuary where nature’s beauty meets thoughtful hospitality. We believe in sustainability, community, and creating moments that last long after your stay.
